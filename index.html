Example 1

In this example you are working on a group project with four other people. The five of you decide to get together wednesday night to really grind out the work. Everyone agrees to head to your crib, and get a few fresh 'zas for that night's grindage. For awhile everything is all that and a bag of chips. Though after awhile the five of you are vibin' some sick tunes. You bring out your boombox, and grab your CD's. Right away one guy (let's call him Kevin) spots your Backstreet's Back. However, someone else (let's call him Tony) spots 'N Sync. It doesn't take long for the convo to get whack. After all this is serious business. Kevin calls Tony a total cruster, and Tony gets ready to start steppin'. Suddenly what was a productive night has dipped straight into static. Now, no one is getting any work done!


So, how could respect fix this? Well, all it would take is someone to tell them to cut! it! out! Whether it is Kevin or Tony, someone can simply say "My bad." Maybe settle on a mutual album, or listen to both albums at some point! Kevin doesn't need to be N' Sync for life, and Tony doesn't need to be grooving in the Backstreets.Through the power of respect though the group can get back to being productive.

Let there be rock!
